Welcome!

I'm an aerospace engineer currently pursuing a master's degree in mechanical engineering (mechatronics). I enjoy alpine climbing, mountain biking, running, flying, photography and many other things. I hold a glider's and private pilot's licence and I have logged 300 hours of flight time so far. On my website I will write mostly about the science projects I work on. The main reason I started a website was to help others searching for solutions for their projects in the same way other people's websites helped me. The major project I am working on right now is the development of a quadcopter flight controller based on the ATmega328P microcontroller.
